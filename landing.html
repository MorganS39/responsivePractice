Uses for Whiskey

Used mainly for dealing with the struggles of learning coding.

Can be used to forget the failures of coding.

Especially useful when dealing with distractions while coding.

Incredibly useful when trying to stop thinking about coding outside of "work" hours.

Attachments that compliment your Whiskey

A large glass in order to help unnecessary trips to refill your drink.

If you don't like the taste of whiskey you can try an assortment of mixing drinks in order to hide the flavor but keep the buzz.

There are a large array of ice cube molds, tumblers, and stir sticks for those that prefer a more fancy experience.

Treatments to over use of Whiskey

The easiest way to prevent pain associated with whiskey is to monitor and regulate your consumption.

For us real people that cannot do that, water is the key to survival.

Food is the next most important thing to surviving your whiskey experience.

If all else fails, sleep and sleep until you feel normal again.
